Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

GriFiid ("we", "us") is a rental listings app that connects renters and landlords in Canada. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using GriFiid you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, date of birth, and password (stored in hashed form). If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google.
  • Profile information: optional phone number and profile photo.
  • Listings: property details, photos, prices, and locations you post.
  • Messages: conversations you have with other users inside the app.
  • Verification documents: if you request a verified-landlord badge, a photo of your government ID. These are stored in a private, access-controlled bucket and used only to verify your identity.
  • Approximate location: if you grant permission, your device location to show nearby listings. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Push token: a device identifier used to deliver message notifications.

2. How we use your information

  • To operate the app: show listings, enable chat between renters and landlords, and send notifications you've enabled.
  • To keep the community safe: reviewing reports, enforcing blocks, and verifying landlord identities.
  • To respond to support requests.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Sharing

  • Other users: your name, profile photo, verified badge, and the listings you post are visible to other users. Your email, birthday, and ID documents are never shown to other users.
  • Service providers: our infrastructure runs on Supabase (database, authentication, file storage) and Expo (app delivery and push notifications). They process data on our behalf under their own security commitments.
  • Legal: we may disclose information if required by law.

4. Data retention

Your data is kept while your account is active. When you delete your account, your profile, listings, favorites, conversations, messages, and verification requests are permanently deleted. Residual copies in backups are purged on the backup rotation schedule.

5. Account deletion

You can delete your GriFiid account and associated data at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → Danger Zone → tap the delete (trash) icon → confirm. Deletion is immediate and permanent.
  • Without the app: email isxul7070@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" from your registered email address, and we will delete your account within 30 days.

Deletion removes your account, profile, listings, favorites, messages, and ID verification data.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under Canada's PIPEDA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at the email below to exercise these rights.

7. Age requirement

GriFiid is for adults only. You must be at least 18 years old to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. Sign-up requires a birthday indicating age 18 or older.

8. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS). Database access is protected by row-level security so users can only access their own private data. ID documents are stored in a private bucket accessible only to you and our review process.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced in the app, and the effective date above will be updated.
